Everyday Things

30 March 2023 | Thursday

Today I'll take you on a journey through my everyday life as an IT intern in the bustling city of Makati, Manila. Join me as I share the ups and downs, challenges, and rewarding experiences of working in the heart of the Philippines' business district. From early mornings to late evenings, let's dive into the rhythm of my daily routine. 7:30 AM - The Start of the Day: The alarm goes off, and I rise from my bed, ready to tackle another day as an IT intern. In Makati, time is precious, so I quickly get dressed, grab my bag, and head out of the apartment. Breakfast is often skipped due to time constraints, but a cup of hot coffee fuels my energy as I brace myself for the day ahead. 8:00 AM - Arrival at the Office: I arrive at the office just in time, blending in with the bustling crowd of professionals making their way to work. Makati's skyline stands tall, an impressive sight that never fails to inspire me. Once inside, I greet my fellow interns and colleagues, and the hum of keyboards and discussions fill the air. 8:15 AM to 12:00 PM - Learning and Assisting: As an intern, my days are filled with opportunities for growth and learning. I dive into various projects and tasks assigned to me, ranging from troubleshooting technical issues to assisting the IT team with software installations and updates. I often find myself researching new technologies and exploring innovative solutions to support the company's operations. During this time, I have the chance to collaborate with experienced professionals who generously share their knowledge and guidance. The office environment is dynamic and fast-paced, demanding both adaptability and quick thinking. Despite the occasional challenges, I find immense satisfaction in contributing to the team's success.

12:00 PM - Lunchtime: Lunchtime is a welcome break from the morning's endeavors. Makati offers a wide range of dining options, from local street food to international cuisines. Whether I opt for a quick bite at a nearby food stall or enjoy a sit-down meal at a cozy restaurant, this time allows me to recharge and connect with colleagues over delicious food and conversations. 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM - Afternoon Engagements: The afternoon hours are typically filled with various engagements, such as attending team meetings, working on individual projects, and collaborating with different departments within the organization. Makati's IT industry is diverse, and it's a privilege to be exposed to a wide range of technologies and domains during my internship. As an IT intern, I encounter complex problem-solving situations that require both technical skills and creative thinking. I often find myself researching and experimenting with innovative approaches, constantly pushing myself to learn and adapt. The atmosphere is vibrant and dynamic, keeping me engaged and motivated throughout the day. 5:00 PM - Heading Back Home: As the workday draws to a close, I pack up my things and bid farewell to my colleagues. Reflecting on the day's achievements and challenges, I make my way back to the apartment. The journey home offers a chance to unwind and mentally transition from the work environment to personal time.

Evenings and weekends provide me with an opportunity to explore Makati further, whether it's checking out the vibrant local nightlife or simply enjoying the tranquility of a nearby park. Makati is a city that never sleeps, and there's always something new to discover. Conclusion: Being an IT intern in Makati, Manila is a thrilling and rewarding experience. From the fast-paced work environment to the diverse challenges and opportunities, each day presents a chance to grow both personally and professionally. As I continue my journey, I am grateful for the chance
